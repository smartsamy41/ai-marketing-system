## Purpose

This document defines the Amazon integration for FBEP.

## Partner Program

Amazon PartnerNet is one of the active affiliate sources.

## Tracking IDs

## Content Rules

Amazon product images must use original Amazon image URLs.

No AI-generated replacement images for Amazon products.

No fake product claims.

No misleading price or availability statements.

## Integration Strategy

Amazon products are treated as affiliate products inside the unified Product Model.

Amazon products can be used as:

- Main product
- Alternative product
- Related product
- Supporting product inside landingpages

## Data Fields

Required fields:

- product_id
- source
- title
- description
- image
- url
- price
- category
- tracking_id
- status

## Landingpage Usage

Amazon products may be combined with:

- Spreadshop designs
- CHECK24 offers
- Tarifcheck products
- Telekom offers

Only when relevant for the visitor.

## Rule

Amazon data must remain compliant with Amazon PartnerNet rules.
