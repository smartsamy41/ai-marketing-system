## Purpose

This document defines SEO, AEO, GEO and AI Search architecture for FBEP.

## Core SEO Layers

- SEO (Search Engine Optimization)
- AEO (Answer Engine Optimization)
- GEO (Generative Engine Optimization)
- AI Ranking Signals

## Crawlers

- Googlebot
- Bingbot
- AI Crawlers
- LLM Indexing Systems

## Ranking Factors

- Content relevance
- Semantic structure
- EEAT (Experience, Expertise, Authority, Trust)
- Structured data
- Page speed
- Mobile usability
- Internal linking
- Backlink quality

## Core Web Vitals

- LCP (Largest Contentful Paint)
- CLS (Cumulative Layout Shift)
- FID / INP (Interaction delay metrics)

## AI Search Strategy

Content must be optimized for:

- Direct answers
- Structured data blocks
- Entity-based SEO
- Question-based content
- Semantic clarity

## Backlink Strategy

- Natural backlinks only
- Content-based link acquisition
- No spam automation
- Authority building through content

## Rule

SEO is not manipulation.

SEO is structured communication for machines and humans.
